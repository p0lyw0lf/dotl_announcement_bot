**Dice help**

Now you can simulate dice rolls without leaving the comfort of your discord window!

Variables:
```
dice size: Number of faces the dice has. Minimum 2. (Default: 6)
```

Commands:

To roll a dice:
```
bud!roll
```

Coming eventually: Dice images.

 * Coin faces for size 2
